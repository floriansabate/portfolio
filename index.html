About Me

My name is Florian Sabaté, I come from the south of France, and I have been passionate about computers and music since I was a child. I studied network security and programming at university, and today I continue in an international college in Thailand.

My main occupations are website design, Android applications, graphics and especially music composition, since it is now my main source of income, in freelancing or ghost production on the Internet.

I am a reserved person, but very involved and hardworker, I work a lot in an autonomous and independent way, and what is more, I am often self-taught. It is mainly in this way that I learned my skills in retouching photos and graphic design as well as electronic music composition. In addition to my professional-looking hobbies, I love history, watching movies, travelling and walking in nature, which is often a source of inspiration for my projects.

I am therefore interested in any type of work or project that allows me to mix my creative side and my computer skills.
